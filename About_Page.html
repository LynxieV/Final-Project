Our mission here at SafeandSound is to help families keep their loved ones safe without having to constantly worry about where they are. The fight against Alzheimer's disease and dementia is not over until a cure is found, and one bracelet means another citizen kept safe. We are a group of four students involved in Girls Who Code passionate about fighting against this deadly disease.
