About Safe and Sound


Helping Keep Families Safe

Our mission here at Safe and Sound is to help families keep their loved ones safe without having to constantly worry about where they are. The fight against Alzheimer's disease and dementia is not over until a cure is found, and one bracelet means another citizen kept safe. We are a group of four students involved in Girls Who Code passionate about fighting against this deadly disease.

Safe and Sound is also expanding from Alzheimer's Disease and Dementia to other known/unknown illness. These ilnesses impair an individual's ability to thought process, it could make the individual know longer have the ability to know where they are and in need of a caretaker.
