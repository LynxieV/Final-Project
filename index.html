Why Safe and Sound

This website was created to allow families to feel safe and secure about the whereabout of their love ones who have Dementia, Altzheimer's Disease, or other diseases, disabilities that leave that family/individual vulnerable. The founders of Safe and Sound wanted to create something that would help ease the stress and worry families may have.

On this website, there is information in the About page that goes further into detail about Safe and Sound and our cause. For further access into our website, create an account of if you already have, log in!
